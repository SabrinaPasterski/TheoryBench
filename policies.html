TheoryBench ✦beta About Launch

Before you download

TheoryBench runs on your Mac. You choose the folder, the accounts, and what leaves your machine.

Control what has access

Choose a paper folder

Open the TeX, figures, bibliography, and notes you already use.

Keep working notes local

Review history and notebook outputs stay local unless exported.

Keep Overleaf in sync

Pull and push paper files while Overleaf remains the shared editor.

Choose when AI can read

AI review may send draft or project text to Claude or Codex. Turn it off to keep drafts local.

What you need installed

TeX

  • Install TeX Live or MacTeX.
  • Local packages may differ from Overleaf.
  • The app reports what it finds.

Wolfram

  • Install Mathematica or Wolfram Engine.
  • Use your own local license.
  • Cells run locally.

Python

  • Uses Python on your machine.
  • Reports missing packages.
  • Keeps outputs with the paper.

macOS

  • First download: macOS.
  • Other packaging comes later.
  • Demo available now.

Beta limits

Overleaf conflicts

If Overleaf and local edits conflict, the app stops before pushing.

Local setup matters

PDFs and computations depend on the tools installed on your Mac.

Early access: the demo is live. The downloadable app is still being packaged and tested.
